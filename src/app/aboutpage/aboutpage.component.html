We are the world's largest university

The Sky Vision College of Digital Technology and Management, Sky Vision College, is an Institute for higher education. The primary motive of Sky Vision College is to “train young men and women able and eager to create and put into action such ideas, methods, techniques and information”. The Institute is a dream come true of its founders Mr. Kishore Kumar Parwani, Mr.Shekhar Kadam, Mr. Munish Sharma, Rachit Karnani an industrialist and Businessman What started in 2021 as a small school, blossomed into a set of colleges for higher education. Over the years, Sky Vision College has provided the highest quality technical education to students from all over Sierra Leone admitted on the basis of merit. Its graduates may be found throughout the world in all areas of engineering, science and commerce. Sky Vision College symbolizes the maturing of Indian technical ability and “can-do” entrepreneurial spirit, especially as derived from the private sector. Sky Vision College located in the Wilberforce campus adjacent to the town of Freetown in Sierra Leone.

Infrastructure

The Sky Vision College is very well equipped with infrastructure and facilities of global standards that covers 30,000 sq. ft. of Campus which adds to the globally competitive dimension of the learning process at the College.

In terms of infrastructure, Sky Vision College provides upgraded classrooms to facilitate teaching through modern tools and techniques. The College has centrally air-conditioned classrooms with Wi-Fi enabled LED projectors, tutorial rooms, library, Smart Interactive Board a state-of the-art seminar room and a fully equipped auditorium. The Sky Vision college library is an empowering unit with a Resource Centre that provides students with various study materials and reference books on digital technology and management. A complete smoke and vehicle free zone, the College provides an eco-friendly environment to its students with the latest initiatives of solar heating, rainwater harvesting, paper recycling and other green initiatives. The College has taken measures to address the need for vigilance and security. The campus is CCTV-enabled to cater to the safety requirements of a well-endowed and equipped campus receiving a huge footfall.
